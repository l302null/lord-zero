Lord Zero

Who is this irresistible creature who has an insatiable love for the macabre? Renowned for his unique on-stage presence the High Priest of The Coven of Zero leaves a trail of blood on every dance floor at every venue he plays. Known for his dark twisted deep dubstep anthems such as House of 1000 Corpses (2.1k) & Killer Klowns (2k) and his track selection mixing deep dubstep , riddim, heavy metal , techno, and other genres takes you on an journey throughout the darkness of your mind.
